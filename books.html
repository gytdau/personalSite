Bookshelf

We live in a utopia of content abundance. This is highly underappreciated! While the natural intuition is that new, fresh information is the most valuable, it's often the opposite: fresh information often has too much noise to be useful. Along those lines, this saying has become a favourite of mine: "If you want new ideas, read old books"

There are some books I thought were good or insightful. Those I've marked with a blue circle.
